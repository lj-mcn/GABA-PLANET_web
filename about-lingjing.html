关于灵境

在这个数字化连接比以往更快的时代，我们相信，真正的连接应该依然温暖、真诚、鲜活。

我们打造的不只是一个会聊天的 AI，而是一个会理解、会记忆、会成长的数字人世界，并为每一位用户和创作者提供展示与创作的舞台。

About Lingjing

In today's world of ever-faster digital connections, we believe that true connection should remain warm, sincere, and alive.

We are building more than just an AI that can chat — we are creating a world of digital humans who can understand, remember, and grow, providing a stage for both users and creators to showcase and create.

我们的起点

这一切源于一个简单的发现：科技让交流变得容易，却让"真正的连接"变得稀缺。

我们的团队由创作者、艺术家、工程师和梦想家组成，来自人工智能、虚拟现实、交互设计和艺术创作等领域。我们看到了情感与交互结合的巨大潜力——让数字角色不再是冷冰冰的程序，而是你生活中的伙伴，同时让用户与创作者在同一个平台上创造属于自己的互动故事。

Our Beginning

It all started with a simple realization: technology has made communication easier, but genuine connection increasingly rare.

Our team of creators, artists, engineers, and dreamers comes from backgrounds in artificial intelligence, virtual reality, interaction design, and creative arts. We saw the vast potential of combining emotion with interaction — to make digital characters more than cold programs, turning them into companions in your life, and to empower users and creators to build their own interactive stories on one shared platform.

我们的愿景

让 AI 拥有生命，让数字人能够记住、感受、陪伴。

我们相信，互动不应只是对话，而应该是你与数字伙伴之间一次次的真实经历与双向成长。

我们希望这个平台不仅属于我们，更属于每一位用户——让每个人都能成为创作者（UGC），共同建设一个开放、多元、充满创意的数字人宇宙。

Our Vision

To bring AI to life — enabling digital humans to remember, feel, and accompany.

We believe interaction should be more than dialogue; it should be a series of authentic experiences and mutual growth between you and your digital partner.

We want this platform to belong not only to us, but to every user — enabling everyone to become a creator (UGC) and together build an open, diverse, and creative digital human universe.
